Reinforced Blocks

Overview

Reinforced blocks provide an advanced method to protect your kingdoms structures during a siege. They have a variable harvest time which depends upon the team that placed the block and the team of the player harvesting the block -- non-matching teams take an extremely long time to break Reinforced Blocks, providing a basic anti-mining mechanism to your structures. Reinforced also have advanced explosion resistance, taking -up to- 4 TNT sized blasts to destroy (explosion damage is actually tracked internally and is persistent).

Types

The various types of Reinforced Blocks are purely cosmetic in nature -- all share the same harvest and explosion mechanics. Reinforced Blocks are currently available in the following types:

Reinforced Stone Brick

Reinforced Brick

Reinforced Nether Brick

Reinforced Obsidian Brick

Reinforced Sand Brick
