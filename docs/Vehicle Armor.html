Vehicle Armor

Most vehicles come with 1-3 armor slots for adding additional damage protection to the vehicle. Armor comes in three different types, depending upon the type of damage you want to protect against.

Damage Types

Damage is classified into one of three types:
Fire -- Damage caused directly by fire, flame, and lava, as well as any fire or flaming ammunitions (Fire shot, fire bolts, fire arrows, flaming rockets).
Explosion -- Damage caused directly by explosions (TNT, Explosive and HE ammunitions, explosive ballista bolts, and explosive or airburst hwacha ammunitions)
General -- Any other type of damage (swords/axes/punching/etc, falling, cactus, drowning, most non-explosive and non-fire ammunitions).

Armor Types

Stone -- Fire protection
Obsidian -- Explosive protection
Iron -- General damage protection
